Isley Royale National Park

Located on an island in Lake Superior, this park offers remote wilderness, rugged coastlines, and opportunities for backpacking, camping, fishing, and wildlife viewing.

Voyageurs National Park

Consisting of four large lakes and more than 900 smaller ones, this park is popular for boating, fishing, and exploring the waterways by canoe or kayak.

Cuayhoga Valley National Park

With a mix of forests, wetlands, and rolling hills, this park offers scenic hiking and biking trails, waterfalls, and historic landmarks like the Ohio & Erie Canal.

Indiana Dunes National Park

Situated on the southern shore of Lake Michigan, this park features towering sand dunes, sandy beaches, and diverse habitats like forests, wetlands, and prairies, making it a great destination for hiking, swimming, and birdwatching.
