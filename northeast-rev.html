Acadia National Park

Located in Maine, this park offers scenic vistas of rugged coastline, granite peaks, and pristine lakes. Visitors can enjoy hiking, biking, camping, and a variety of water-based activities.

Shenandoah National Park

Situated in Virginia, this park features the scenic Blue Ridge Mountains and the famous Skyline Drive. The park offers hiking trails, campgrounds, and stunning views of the Shenandoah Valley.

Delaware Water Gap National Park

Located on the border of Pennsylvania and New Jersey, the Delaware Water Gap offers visitors the chance to hike, swim, boat, and fish in a stunning natural setting.

Cape Cod National Park

This Massachusetts park is famous for its sandy beaches, dunes, and salt marshes. Visitors can explore historic lighthouses, bike along scenic paths, and observe a variety of wildlife.
